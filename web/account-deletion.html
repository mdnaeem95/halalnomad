Delete your account

You can request deletion of your HalalNomad account and all associated data at any time. We'll process the request within 30 days.

Email us to request deletion

Email hello@halalnomad.travel from the address tied to your account so we can verify it's really you.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (display name, avatar, points, tier)
  • Photos you've uploaded for places
  • Reviews you've written
  • Verifications and reports you've submitted
  • Your push notification token and notification history
  • Your authentication record (email + password)

What we may retain

For audit and platform-integrity reasons, we retain a small amount of anonymised data after deletion:

  • Aggregate place data — places you originally added stay live for other users, but the link back to your identity is removed.
  • Anonymised analytics events — already de-identified before deletion; cannot be traced back to you.

We never retain personally identifiable information after a deletion request is honoured.

Timeline

Most requests are processed within 7 days. The 30-day window is a hard cap (per GDPR Article 17), and we'll only ever take that long if the deletion request is contested or unclear.

Questions

Email hello@halalnomad.travel — happy to walk you through what will and won't be deleted before you commit.
